About Me

❮ ❯

Music has been an integral part of my entire life. From song-writing to recording and producing to live mixing and performances, I enjoy the entire process and enjoy working with all the industry professionals along the way. I've recorded at some of the biggest studios in Australia, like The Grove, and toured overseas.

Our band, Salt+Light, released an EP with two #1 hit singles, You're The Only One and My Heart, which can be previewed in my portfolio page.

In today's evolving era of modern music, keyboardists are required to dive into the deep end of the virtual software instrument world. Having strong sound design skills is an unspoken expectation to deliver high fidelity sounds that add elements which elevate and enhance the song. Creating custom automation and mapping scripts enables the user to wield the power of computational processing and manipulation to achieve feats that are not humanly possible.

I'm also a car enthusiast, I enjoy tinkering with things, understanding how it all works, pulling things apart and building something from the ground up. It's a delicate fine art to find the perfect balance between performance and efficiency. Optimizing power without compromising practicality.
