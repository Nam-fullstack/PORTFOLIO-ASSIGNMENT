About Me

❮ ❯

Music has been an integral part of my entire life. From song-writing to recording and producing to live mixing and performances, I enjoy the entire process and enjoy working with all the industry professionals along the way. I've recorded at some of the biggest studios in Australia, like The Grove, and toured in multiple countries overseas. Our band, Salt+Light, recently released an EP with two #1 hit singles, You're The Only One and My Heart, which can be previewed in my portfolio page.

With today's music shifting towards electronic virtual instruments, it was a steep learning curve to master using audio software platforms and sound design to create high fidelity sound elements to enhance every aspect of a song. One of the most difficult challenges, yet most rewarding, was learning how to create automation and mapping scripts. In doing so, I was able to wield the power of computational processing and manipulation to change over a dozen parameters with a turn of a knob or push of a single button. Thus enabling the user to have absolute control over sound manipulation without needing to resort to inflexible backing tracks.

I'm also an avid car enthusiast. I enjoy tinkering with things, understanding how it all works, pulling things apart and building it all from the ground up. Finding the perfect balance between performance and efficiency is a delicate art - optimizing power without compromising practicality.
